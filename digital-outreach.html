Digital Outreach

Extending the reach of CATS Insights beyond the classroom - connecting with audiences, sharing our work, and building a visible digital footprint.

Our Digital Strategy

CATS Insights maintains an active presence across multiple digital platforms. Each channel serves a distinct purpose: from sharing educational content and behind-the-scenes updates on Instagram, to hosting our codebase and technical infrastructure on GitHub, to publishing long-form video content on YouTube. Together, these platforms demonstrate our commitment to making our work publicly accessible and transparent.

Below you will find direct links to every official CATS Insights channel. Judges and visitors are encouraged to explore each platform to see the breadth and consistency of our digital outreach.

Educational Content Showcase

We equip our team members with the skills and opportunity to create engaging educational multimedia. Below is an example of the communicative content our members learn to produce to bring science to public audiences.

Microwaves and Metal: The Science Explained

This dynamic explainer visualises the complex physical chemistry behind why placing metal inside a microwave causes plasma arcing. By combining clear scripting, engaging visuals, and robust scientific fact-checking, this piece exemplifies our commitment to making advanced STEM concepts accessible. It also serves as a tangible demonstration of the digital, communication, and multimedia skillsets that CATS Insights members regularly develop and apply.

Why Digital Outreach Matters

Digital outreach is not an afterthought: it is an integral part of the CATS Insights mission. By maintaining a consistent, multi-platform presence, we ensure that the work produced by our contributors reaches audiences far beyond the school gates. Every post, video, and repository is an act of public science communication, demonstrating that student-led academic content can meet professional standards and deserve a public audience.
