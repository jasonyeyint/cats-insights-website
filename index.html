CATS Insights

Real Curiosity. Real Writing. Real Kent.

CATS Insights is a student-run academic publication from The Worthgate School, Canterbury. We give students the platform, the mentoring, and the infrastructure to produce work of genuine academic excellence and the freedom to pursue the questions that fascinate them most.

"I built this on a simple conviction: given structure, direction, and purpose, a student's intellect will always exceed what anyone expects of it." — Kyal Sin Ye Yint, Founder & President
