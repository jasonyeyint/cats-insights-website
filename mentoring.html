Baseline vs. Recent Submissions

The metrics are powered by highly intensive review cycles. Below are verified feedback extracts sourced directly from the Level 2 Writing Assessments logged in the metrics tracker, proving how our mentors actively push writers to adapt their academic complexity to the correct audience level.

Student A (Year 12)

Level 2 Formatting Transition

"You have demonstrated a strong ability to translate Expert (Level 1) density into an Educated Non-Specialist (Level 2) format while retaining scientific precision. However, there are a few formatting rules mandated... that you missed."

Score Improvement: +70% over baseline.

Student B (Year 12)

Jargon Simplification

"You made an excellent strategic decision to completely translate complex jargon (like 'endothelial dysfunction' and 'atherogenesis') into plain English rather than just defining them."

Score Improvement: +80% over baseline.

Student C (Year 12)

Citation & Authority Integrity

"You made a massive leap forward by successfully integrating your in-text citations and linking them to a highly credible bibliography. However, in your effort to define every technical term, you created a single, incredibly dense run-on sentence."

Score Improvement: +60% over baseline.
