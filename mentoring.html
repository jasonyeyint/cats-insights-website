Mentoring: Building Better Science Writers

The Editorial Workflow

Our mentoring system is built directly into our operational workflow. Junior writers receive structured, actionable feedback from Lead Writers at multiple stages.

Stage 1: Writer submits Draft 1 (Google Drive)
↓
Stage 2: Lead Editor Reviews (3-Level Framework + PEEL)
↓
Stage 3: Feedback Summary Template Returned (Teams)
↓
Stage 4: Writer Revises & Submits Draft 2
↓
Stage 5: Final Review (Editorial-ready / Needs revision)
↓
Stage 6: Editorial-ready articles enter QA Pipeline
