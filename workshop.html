RSC Category 3: STEM School Event

Writing Chemistry for Non-Chemists

Date

12 November 2025

Location

The Worthgate School

Duration

45 minutes

Facilitator

Jason Ye Yint (Founder & President)

Attendees

45 CATS Insights members from Years 10–13

Learning Objectives

  1. Distinguish between 3 audience levels (Expert, Educated Non-Specialist, General Public).
  2. Apply PEEL paragraph structure to science writing.
  3. Self-assess using the Three-Test Framework (Specialist Test, Peer Test, Citation Test).
