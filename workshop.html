Writing Chemistry for Non-Chemists

The Foundational STEM Communication Masterclass

Masterclass Overview

This masterclass taught students the essential framework for communicating complex scientific concepts to non-specialists. Focus areas included controlling scientific jargon, structuring arguments using the 3-Level framework, and maintaining academic rigour while ensuring mass accessibility.

UNESCO Learning Planet Day Workshop

International Recognition & School-Registered Event

Event Overview

On the behest of The Worthgate School, CATS Insights prepared a workshop on academic writing, research, how to read research papers, how to extract information without plagiarising, and essentially a masterclass covering a plethora of different academic topics to teach students.
