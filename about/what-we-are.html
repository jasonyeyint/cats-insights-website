What Is CATS Insights?

A student-run academic publication built from scratch, for purpose.

Where It Came From

CATS Insights was founded in late 2025 by Jason (Kyal Sin Ye Yint), a Year 12 student at The Worthgate School, Canterbury. It started with a straightforward observation: students had no structured platform to pursue genuine intellectual work, no place to research a question that fascinated them, produce something of publishable quality, and develop the skills that matter most beyond the classroom. Rather than wait for one to appear, Kyal Sin (Jason) Ye Yint built it. Drawing on first-hand experience with student-led publishing, the organisation was designed from the ground up with the infrastructure that most school publications lack: formal governance, a documented editorial pipeline, structured mentoring, and a succession plan that ensures the publication outlasts any single team and is accessible to all students who possess the curiosity and determination to learn.

Mission Statement

"We built CATS Insights on a single conviction: that a student's intellect, given the right structure, will always exceed what anyone expects of it. Everything we publish is proof."

What CATS Insights Offers

Every member holds a real role with real accountability. Writers research and draft to publication standard. Editors audit for accuracy, structure, and academic integrity. Designers produce branded visual content to documented specifications. Every piece of published work carries the contributor's name and every piece passes through a multi-stage editorial pipeline before it earns that right.

The skills this builds, academic writing, independent research, editorial judgement, design thinking, and leadership are precisely the ones that university admissions tutors look for and rarely find evidence of.

What We Are Not

We are NOT... ...a school magazine.
We are an academic publication with peer review and quality assurance.
We are NOT... ...a credentials factory.
Contributors produce work they would genuinely read, not essays manufactured for a personal statement.
We are NOT... ...exclusive.
No prior experience required. Only curiosity, reliability, and a willingness to learn.
We are NOT... ...temporary.
Every system, document, and framework is built to outlast the people who created it.
