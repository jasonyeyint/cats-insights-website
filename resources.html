CATS Skills

Three supplementary workshop courses covering critical operational skills: professional documentation, DofE Award portfolio building, and AI responsible use — all integrated with the STAR method and SMART goal frameworks.

3 Courses 7 Lessons 7 Hours Total

  • 1. Scribe & Achievement Logging (2 lessons) — Meeting minutes, 5 W’s framework, STAR method, personal achievement logging.
