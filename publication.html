Official Publications

Distinguishing high-caliber scholarship from the experimental playground.

While The CATS Insights Journal (our blog) serves as a practice playground for emerging writers, the Official Publications represent the absolute peak of student achievement in CATS Insights. These works undergo a rigorous 6-stage editorial pipeline, faculty audit, and professional design phase. Students will have to dedicate a considerable volume of time in order to create a piece of excellent academic writing with all the skills and experiences developed from the academic development workshop courses.
